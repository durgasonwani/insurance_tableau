# 📊 Insurance Tableau Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an interactive **Insurance Opportunity Performance and Revenue Analysis Dashboard** created using **Tableau**.

The dashboard analyzes opportunity stages, revenue performance, top opportunities, products, employees, and meeting dates. It helps users understand which opportunities and products generate the highest revenue.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze opportunity performance by stage  
- Track revenue amount across opportunities  
- Identify top-performing opportunities  
- Compare revenue by insurance product  
- Monitor open opportunities and total opportunities  
- Analyze employee-wise opportunities  
- Filter data by meeting year  

---

## 📊 Dashboard Insights

### 💰 Stage by Revenue
This chart shows revenue generated at different opportunity stages.

- **Qualify Opportunity** has the highest revenue.
- **Negotiate** has lower revenue compared to the qualifying stage.
- **Propose Solution** has the lowest revenue.

### 🏆 Top 4 Opportunities
The dashboard displays the top four opportunities based on revenue:

- Fire  
- EL-Group Mediclaim  
- DB-Mega Policy  
- CVP GMC  

### 📈 Top 5 Policies
This visual compares the top five policies based on revenue amount.

- BE-Mega Policy  
- CVP GMC  
- DB-Mega Policy  
- DB-Terrorism  
- EL-Group Mediclaim  

### 🧩 Opportunity by Product
The treemap shows revenue contribution by product category.

Products included:

- Mediclaim  
- Constructions & Infrastructure  
- Engineering  
- Financial Lines  
- Marine Hull  
- Marine Cargo  

### 👥 Open and Total Opportunities
The dashboard displays:

- Open Opportunities  
- Total Opportunities  

### 🎛️ Interactive Filters
Users can filter the dashboard using:

- Year of Meeting Date  
- Employee Name  
- Opportunity Stage  
- Product Category
